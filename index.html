Research

My primary research interest is in using machine learning to discover novelty, change, or features of interest in remotely sensed data. I have worked with grayscale, color, and multispectral images from the Mars Science Laboratory Curiosity Mastcam camera, the High Resolution Imaging Science Experiment (HiRISE) and Context Camera (CTX) cameras on the Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter, and the RapidEye satellites operated by Planet.
